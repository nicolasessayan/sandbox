Install CodeReef

CodeReef requires minimal dependencies (Python 2.7+ or 3.x, PIP and Git) and can be setup and used very quickly from your command line. To dig further, you can read our documentation.

1 Install

pip install codereef

2 Launch

cr start

3 Use

Components and AI solutions can be installed and configured with the specific commands mentioned in our libraries. For instance, TensorFlow (in its version 1.9.0 for CPU) can be installed as follow:

cr install tensorflow-1.9.0-cpu

in AI innovation

CodeReef is an open platform to share and interconnect components of AI systems (models, datasets, frameworks, other dependencies...) from R&D projects under a common format. We enable thus automated, portable, reproducible and reusable workflows. For more details, you can consult our mission statement.

Automated

Setup functional workflows in minutes instead of weeks through automation of tedious tasks (e.g detection of dependencies, installation of missing packages, compiling, tuning, ...).

Reproducible

Share the composition of your workflows so that others know exactly how you produced your results. Obtain fair and reliable measurements to compare solutions.

Portable

Adapt your workflows so that your solutions can be deployed on any hardware or operating system. Updates their components with few modifications.

Reusable

Save time, energy and money by recycling your components to build other solutions. Exchange them with the Community for efficient and sustainable R&D.
